About Me

Hi there, my name is Francisco Tamayo, Fullstack developer with MERN, Flutter and hardware, with more than 3 years of experience creating and collaborating with real projects. I started at 11 years old, building electronic circuits, I was very curious to know how all those little pieces could do such incredible things. I studied each component on my own on Internet and little by little I got to know people who knew more than I did and were willing to share their knowledge with me. I remember that the first circuit I did on my own was a "laser alarm", like spy movies. It took me a long time to do math for it to work. At 14 I was able to buy my first Arduino and the person who sold it to me became my mentor and then my friend. It was when I discovered the world of software and realized that this was the future. As I progressed through Arduino, I was exploring parallel worlds like internet protocols, mobile apps, and web pages. That's when I put the arduino aside for a while and entered the world of software development. I learned a lot and very varied, from the web pages, to the server configuration. When I was 16 I joined a group from a university in my city with which we participated in two StartUps contests where we reached the semifinals. I was the youngest of the team :). At 18 I was able to work formally as a software developer and since then I have participated in numerous projects and have gained a lot of experience. At the same age, I also entered La Salle University to study Software Engineering, which I am currently studying. Now I work in 3 areas, FullStack with MERN, mobile developer with Flutter and hardware developer. I like changes, that's why I constantly train with the latest technologies that come onto the market and my mission is to be able to call myself a "professional". I am working for that.
